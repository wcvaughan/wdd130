Overview

Purpose

As a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, Alaska provides ample opportunity for filling every niche of recreation. This project aims to appeal to those investing in their once in a lifetime Alaskan vacation.

Audience

The target audience for this website are outdoor enthusiasts who are planning vacations to Alaska. Who are the target customers? This group may range from young adults seeking memorable Alaskan recreation to retired men and women who now travel and seek new and exciting experiences. What are their needs that aren't being met? Many who plan a vacation to Alaska have specific ideas on how they plan to spend it. Whether this is hunting or sightseeing. Either way the need of these people is to have memorable experiences to take back to their loved ones. How will my audience usually access my site? It is likely a portion will access this page through desktop computers, however the majority are likely to access it through mobile devices.
